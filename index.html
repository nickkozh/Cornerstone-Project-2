Resource Balance

You're in charge of keeping a city's electricity and water running. Can you keep both in the green?

How it works:
Move the sliders (or turn the physical dials) to control how much electricity and water you use.

Green zone (10–35%) — the resource recharges and you earn cards.
Below 10% — the resource slowly leaks. Stay here too long and it locks up!
Above 35% — the resource drains fast. Hit 0% and the lights go out!

Buy upgrades with your cards to make the city more efficient.
